About Shishukunj MUN

The Shishukunj Model United Nations conference is one of the biggest conferences of its kind. For its fourth iteration, to be held on September 27, 2018, to September 30, 2018, Shishukunj MUN offers a one of a kind opportunity to students across India by stimulating an academic cum extra-curricular experience for the delegates to enjoy and be inspired by. Hosted by and at the Shishukunj International School, Indore, Shishukunj MUN promises to be an enriching and unforgettable experience for all.
