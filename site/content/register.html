Registration Timeline

Registration at Shishukunj MUN 2018 will be done in two phases.

  1. Priority Registrations: For all early-bird delegations, who apply and submit payments before July 31, will be given preference in country allotments.
  2. Normal Registrations: After July 31, the registration will be considered on a rolling basis. All payments and registrations are to be done before August 20 for successful enrollment at Shishukunj MUN 2018.

You can access further details here.

Registration Fees

The following fees apply to all domestic delegations/delegates of Shishukunj MUN 2018:

  1. A school registration fee of INR 4000.00 per delegation- Includes 1 faculty advisor fee plus 15% service tax and 0.5% Swacch Bharat Cess.
  2. A delegate fee of INR 4000.00 per delegate - Includes conference participation, access to conference events, access to pre-conference training webinars and speaker sessions, meals during conference, airport/train/bus station drop and pick up facility, and 15% service tax and 0.5% Swacch Bharat Cess.
  3. A faculty advisor/observer fee of INR 3000.00 per additional faculty advisor - Includes conference faculty advisor agenda and participation, access to all conference events, meals during conference, airport/train/bus station drop and pick up facility, and 15% service tax and 0.5% Swachh Bharat Cess.

For more information regarding payment details and refunds, click here.

For any other assistance, please contact USG: Finance and Operations, Bhakti Badlani at operations@shishukunjmun.com, or Conference Director, Garvit Zalani at confdir@shishukunjmun.com.
